Luna.mind

A psychotherapeutic clinic that helps existing patients and works to develop the craft of psychological care with the help of innovative technologies

Pain points

The clinic management is not up-to-date and not digitalized. No one is aware of the strengths of this clinic. No online treatment option

Process

Workshop with a client, user flow, competitor analysis, wireframes, prototype, usability testing, design system, adaptive layout design, development support

Result

The company declared itself in the digital space, which allowed it to stand out among competitors, especially in Covid times. Created 4 roles for convenient online management of clinic resources for group and individual therapy

A therapist has all the necessary patient records to conduct group and individual sessions online

Records contain the patient's personal information for the admin and also the treatment trajectory for the therapist

Document management automation has significantly increased the efficiency of work in the clinic

Now it is possible to quickly fill out documentation with already pre-filled data individually for each patient
